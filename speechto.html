Troubleshooting Speech Recognition Errors

Encountering errors during speech recognition is common. Here’s how to address them:

  • No Speech Detected: Ensure you’re speaking clearly and check for background noise. Adjust the no-speech timeout setting.
  • Microphone Access Denied: Enable microphone permissions in your browser settings and try again.
  • Network Issues: Verify your internet connection, as speech recognition relies on online services.
  • Browser Incompatibility: Use Google Chrome, which fully supports the Web Speech API.
  • Language Not Supported: Select a supported language from the dropdown menu.

Our tool provides clear error messages to guide you through resolving these issues, ensuring a smooth experience.
